Format of report

- Cover Page: Includes title of the project, author name and  publicayion date, any contact details

- Outline: Table of contents: which gives the readers an overview of the contents

- Exceutive Summary: Abstract

- Introdcution: What is the report about, who is the report for, what will the reader gain by reading this report

- Methodology: What dara sources were used, how was the data collected
 
- Results: Main findings and implications using visuals from data as evidence

- Discusion: time to tell the story of overall findings and implications

- Conclusion: Tell about the conclusions you have arrived on

- Appendix: Additional info that you deemed is important
